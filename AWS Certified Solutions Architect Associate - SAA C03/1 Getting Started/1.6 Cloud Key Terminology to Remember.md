# ☁️ Cloud Key Terminology to Remember

---

# 🔑 Core Cloud Concepts

---

## 📈 Elasticity
> Ability to automatically scale resources up or down based on demand

- Scale **up** → handle high traffic  
- Scale **down** → save cost during low usage  

✅ No manual intervention needed  
✅ Efficient resource usage  

---

## ⚡ Agility
> Ability to move fast and respond quickly to business needs

### Key Aspects:
- Quickly deliver software  
- Quickly provision infrastructure  

✅ Faster innovation  
✅ Faster time-to-market  

---

## 🌍 Global Reach & Geo Distribution
> Ability to deploy applications across multiple regions worldwide

### Benefits:
- Expand into new markets easily  
- Deploy closer to users  
- Improve performance  

✅ Global presence in minutes  

---

## ✅ Availability
> Ensures applications are accessible when users need them

- High uptime  
- Minimal downtime  
- Reliable systems  

✅ Critical for business continuity  

---

## ⏱️ Latency
> Time taken for a request to travel between user and server

### Key Idea:
- Lower latency = faster user experience  

### How to Reduce?
- Serve users from **nearby locations (regions)**  

✅ Improves application performance  

---

# 📊 Important Business & Cost Terms

---

## 💰 Capital Expenditure (CapEx)
> Large upfront investment in infrastructure

### Examples:
- Buying servers  
- Building data centers  

❌ High initial cost  
❌ Requires long-term planning  

---

## 🔁 Operational Expenditure (OpEx)
> Ongoing cost of renting and using resources

### Examples:
- Paying for cloud servers  
- Subscription-based services  

✅ Pay as you use  
✅ Flexible and scalable  

---

## 💵 Pay-as-You-Go
> Pay only for the resources you actually use

### Concept:
- Convert **CapEx → OpEx**

✅ No upfront investment  
✅ Cost aligned with usage  

---

## 📉 Economies of Scale
> Buying resources in large quantities reduces cost per unit

### Cloud Reality:
- Providers buy infrastructure at massive scale  
- Cost savings passed to customers  

✅ Lower overall pricing  

---

# 🏋️ Avoid Undifferentiated Heavy Lifting

---

## 📌 Definition
> Tasks that are necessary but **do not differentiate your business**

### Examples:
- Managing servers  
- Maintaining infrastructure  
- Handling hardware issues  

---

## ⚠️ Problem
- Consumes time and resources  
- Does not add business value  

---

## ☁️ Cloud Benefit
- Cloud provider handles infrastructure  
- You focus on core business  

---

### 🎯 Example
**Insurance Company:**
- Focus on **better premium calculation**
- Not on managing servers

---

# 📌 Final Summary

Key cloud terminology helps understand:

- 📈 Scaling (Elasticity)  
- ⚡ Speed (Agility)  
- 🌍 Expansion (Global Reach)  
- ✅ Reliability (Availability)  
- ⏱️ Performance (Latency)  
- 💰 Cost Models (CapEx vs OpEx)  
- 📉 Pricing Advantage (Economies of Scale)  
- 🏋️ Efficiency (Avoid Heavy Lifting)
